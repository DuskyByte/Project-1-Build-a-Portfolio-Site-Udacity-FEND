Who am I?

Hi there, I am Jeff. I am 35 years old and recently engaged to my beatiful fiancé, Crystal. I am one of the lucky few that made it through into the 2nd Phase of the Grow with Google Scholarship Challenge. I hope to one day work for Google or another large software company as a Front End Web Developer. As for my work with coding, well it has been going on for a few years now.

I first started coding with Microsoft Basic in the 1990s. When I joined the Air Force in 2001 I gained a little more experience with code, but it was always just a bit of dabbling here and there. It wasn't until recently, when I was awarded the Grow with Google Scholarship, that I actually sat down and really started to learn how to code in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
